The Story

Rooted in Texas.
Bloomed in Atlanta.

Rolonda Rochelle is a Texas native who made Atlanta her home in July of 1993 — drawn to its thriving film and television industry. But it wasn't until after 40 that she made the decision to defy the odds and follow her dream of becoming an actress.

Studying under master coaches including Bill Duke, Nevaina Rhodes and Kristen Shaw, Rolonda mastered the Meisner and Stanislavsky techniques while learning the business of the craft. She stepped away briefly to focus on real estate and self-development — returning to the industry with a deeper perspective, a stronger voice and an undeniable presence.

Today Rolonda is not only a working actress with credits spanning film, television and web series — she's a pro-aging advocate with over 1 million followers, a brand ambassador, and the founder of Silver Krown Productions. She is living proof that the best chapters haven't been written yet.

"You're never too old to set another goal or dream another dream. Just go for it!"
— Rolonda Rochelle

Pro-Aging Advocate

Age is Not a
Limitation.

Since 2016 Rolonda has been one of the most vocal and visible advocates for pro-aging — challenging ageism in the entertainment industry and in everyday life.

With over 1 million Facebook followers, she has built a community of people who refuse to let age define what's next for them. Her message is simple and powerful: exhale, explore and experience life through a different lens.

As a brand ambassador, social media influencer and keynote presence, Rolonda brings this message to stages, campaigns and screens — inspiring generations of women to walk in their truth and live without limitations.

"Don't let age, your past, or circumstances define what's next for you."
— Rolonda Rochelle
